Parent Guide & FAQ

What age is this appropriate for?

Baby TapTime is designed for toddlers aged 6 months to 3 years, with interactions that grow with your child's development.

Is it completely safe for children?

Yes! The app works 100% offline, contains no ads, collects no data, and has no external links or in-app purchases that could be accidentally triggered.

How do I adjust the settings?

Tap the settings icon (visible to adults) to adjust sound volume, haptic feedback strength, and visual effect intensity.

Will this help my child's development?

The app encourages hand-eye coordination, cause-and-effect learning, and fine motor skill development through age-appropriate touch interactions.

Does it work without internet?

Yes! Baby TapTime is completely offline and doesn't require any internet connection to function.

Are there any ongoing costs?

No subscriptions or in-app purchases. It's a one-time purchase that your family can enjoy forever.
